# Plan 6 - Monetization Strategy & SaaS Revenue Model

> How Malkiat generates sustainable, diversified revenue across consumer, B2B, and transactional channels.

---

## Revenue Model Philosophy

Malkiat should never depend on a single revenue stream. The strategic goal is a hybrid model combining:

1. **B2B SaaS subscriptions** (recurring, predictable)
2. **Transaction-based fees** (aligned with value delivered)
3. **Advertising + placements** (marketplace monetization)
4. **Data & services** (high-margin, scalable)

This mirrors how Zillow (Premier Agent + Rentals + Mortgage), Redfin (brokerage + mortgage), and Bayut (Profolio™ SaaS + featured listings) all generate revenue — multiple streams reinforcing each other.

## Revenue Stream 2: Listing Boost Credits (Marketplace Advertising)

**Model:** Pay-per-use credit system. Credits are purchased in bundles and spent on boosting listings.

### Credit Types

| Credit Type | Effect | Price (PKR) |
|---|---|---|
| **Hot Credit** | "Hot" badge + priority in discovery feed for 7 days | 800 |
| **Featured Credit** | Top 3 placement in city/area search for 7 days | 2,000 |
| **Refresh Credit** | Manually refresh listing freshness timer | 200 |
| **Story Credit** | Short-form video slot on homepage/app feed for 3 days | 1,500 |
| **Spotlight Credit** | Homepage hero banner (limited slots, city-specific) | 5,000 |

### Credit Bundles

| Bundle | Credits Included | Price (PKR) | Savings |
|---|---|---|---|
| Starter Pack | 5 Hot + 2 Refresh | 5,000 | 10% |
| Growth Pack | 10 Hot + 5 Featured + 5 Refresh | 22,000 | 15% |
| Power Pack | 20 Hot + 10 Featured + 10 Refresh + 2 Story | 40,000 | 20% |

**Notes:**
- All boosted listings are transparently labeled "Featured" or "Sponsored" to users
- Credits never expire (builds willingness to buy in bulk)
- Free tier agents can still purchase credits à la carte (no subscription required)

**Revenue projection:**
- 500 agents buying PKR 5,000 in credits/month avg = PKR 2.5M/month

## Revenue Stream 4: Short-Term Rental Commission

**Model:** Commission on each completed booking.

- Malkiat charges **12–15% guest service fee** (charged to guest, standard in market)
- Malkiat charges **3–5% host fee** (deducted from host payout)
- Combined take: 15–20% of booking value per transaction

**Revenue projection:**
- At 500 bookings/month averaging PKR 5,000/night × 2 nights avg stay = PKR 5M GMV
- At 15% take rate = PKR 750K/month from STR bookings by Month 18

| Service | Price (PKR) | Delivery |

| Basic Inspection Report | 5,000 | Photos + condition notes within 72h |
| Full Verification Report | 12,000 | Photos + video + legal doc review + neighborhood assessment within 48h |

## Revenue Stream 6: Home Finance Lead Referrals

**Model:** Affiliate/referral commission from bank/finance partners.

- **Mortgage eligibility tool:** User fills form → lead routed to partner bank
- **Bank pays:** PKR 2,000–5,000 per qualified lead OR ~0.5–1% of sanctioned loan amount
- No upfront cost to user; high intent = high conversion for banks

**Revenue projection:**
- 1,000 finance leads/month × PKR 3,000 avg referral fee = PKR 3M/month at scale
- Commission on sanctioned loans (if bank partner pays %): very high potential ceiling

## Pricing Principles

1. **Free tier is mandatory.** Agents won't pay without trying. Free → Starter is the conversion funnel.
2. **Credits lower the adoption barrier.** An agent who won't pay PKR 6,000/month will happily spend PKR 800 on one Hot Credit to test value.
3. **Annual billing builds LTV.** Always offer 2 months free on annual to lock in revenue and reduce churn.
4. **Transparent boost labeling builds user trust.** Unlike Zameen, where users can't tell what's paid vs organic, Malkiat labels everything — this is a feature, not a limitation.
5. **Never gate search quality behind payment.** The discovery experience must be excellent for all users. Monetization happens around the edges (CRM, boosts, campaigns), not at the search layer.

## SaaS Business Model KPIs

| KPI | Description | Target (Month 18) |
|---|---|---|
| MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue) | Subscription revenue only | PKR 5M+ |
| ARR | Annual run rate | PKR 60M+ |
| Net Revenue Retention | Expansion - churn | > 110% (expansion exceeds churn) |
| Churn Rate | Paying agents canceling/month | < 5%/month |
| CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost) | Cost to acquire one paying agent | < PKR 3,000 |
| LTV (Lifetime Value) | Revenue per paying agent over their lifetime | > PKR 60,000 |
| LTV:CAC Ratio | Business viability metric | > 20:1 |
| Payback Period | Months to recover CAC | < 2 months |
